Embroidery For Beginners – What Supplies Will You Need?

You Have you always wanted to try embroidery, but you weren’t sure where to start? I’m here to share all of the basics with you, so you know what resources you need and then how to get started.

Let’s Get Started


The most important thing to remember is that you don’t need to have the best of everything to get stitching. It’s true that there are many tools, notions and supplies that you can purchase that will make your sewing easier and will also improve the finish. But when you are just starting out, it’s wonderful to be able
to use the resources that you have
on hand and buy the extra supplies as you
become more proficient.

The Basics

Fabric And Stabiliser

It’s always best to go for a plain or a neutral fabric. The fabric colour can really make a difference to the finished look of the design so consider the effect you’re going for.

Hoop

Placing your fabric in an embroidery hoop can really help you maintain an even tension while you stitch.


Needles

Selecting needles for embroidery is a personal choice. Consider the type of floss, thread, and stitches you'll use when making your choice.

Floss

Choose 6-stranded cotton floss, such as DMC, for your hand embroidery. It comes in a variety of colors and is sold in skeins.
